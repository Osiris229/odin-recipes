Jollof Rice

Description

This is a famous West African dish, thats eaten in every West African country in one way or another. Its a famous daish becasuse its the perfect side dish to many different foods, but it can also be eaten on its on and be considered a main dish. This is a more flavored and better tasting option than just eating plain white rice with another food or just eating white rice on its on

All West African countries think that they have the best version of the Jollof rice, because the dish is slightly different or prepared in different steps between all the countries. One of the famous arguments between countries regarding their Jollof, which can be seen online, is between Ghana and Nigeria. Both countries do believe that their Jollof is the best in the World, and people from both countriess do think they are the ones that are right.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Water
  • Tomatos
  • Onion
  • Bell Pepper
  • Tomato Paste
  • Paprika Seasonig
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic Powder
  • Cumin
  • Corriander
  • Bay leaves
  • Red Cayenne Pepper
  • Magi seasoning

Steps

  1. Chop one onion, into small cubes, take another onion and cut in into medium sized pieces, then do the same with 3 tomatos and 1 bell pepper as you did with the second onion.
  2. Secondly warm up a hot Pot and put in a little bit of oil and put in your small cut onions and let them slowly cook on medium to medium high heat. (This should take around 5 minutes)
  3. Next whilst your onions are cooking take your bigger cut Onions,bell pepper and tomatos and use a blender to blend them into a liquid.
  4. After your Onions have been cooking for around 5 minutes, add about 2 tablespoons of tomato paste and mix it in well with the cut onions and let cook for around 2 minutes.
  5. Now that the onions and paste have been cooking together for 2 minutes, add your blended mixture.
  6. Mix it well with the onions and paste and now add your seasonings to your likings.
  7. After having added the seasonings and mixing them well in, add some more oil and turn the heat dow to low to medium and close the lid on the pot and let the tomato based sauce cook for around 10-15 minutes. Until it has an intense red color and looks fully cooked.
  8. Whilst your sauce is cooking, take your desired amount and choice of rice and wash it thourgly, until the water runs clear.
  9. Now that your sauce has been cooking and has a nice intense red color, add your washed rice and some water to the pot and mix it in well. Make sure your heat is at low and close the lid.
  10. Let the rice cook on low for 15 minutes or more depending on how low th heat is, you can mix it once or twice during that time ensuring that it wont stick to the bottom of the pot and burn.
  11. After your rice is done cooking, you can add a can or two of sardines, to intense the flavors.(Optional)
